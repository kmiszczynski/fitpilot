## Exercise 5 of 5

### Mountain Climbers

**Goal:**
Perform as many controlled repetitions as you can in **45 seconds**.

**How to do it:**

* Start in a high plank position
* Bring one knee toward your chest, then switch legs
* Keep your core engaged and hips stable
* Move at a steady, controlled pace

**Tips:**

* Focus on control, not speed
* Breathe steadily
* Stop if you feel pain or dizziness
