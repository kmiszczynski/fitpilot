## Exercise 3 of 5

### Reverse Snow Angels

**Goal:**
Perform as many controlled repetitions as you can in **45 seconds**.

**How to do it:**

* Lie face down on the floor
* Keep your forehead lightly on the ground
* Lift your arms slightly off the floor
* Move them slowly from overhead to your sides and back

**Tips:**

* Squeeze your shoulder blades together
* Move with control, not speed
* Stop if you feel discomfort in your shoulders or neck
