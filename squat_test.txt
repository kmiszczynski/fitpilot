## Exercise 1 of 5

### Squats

**Goal:**
Perform as many squats as you can in **60 seconds**.

**How to do it:**

* Stand with feet about shoulder-width apart
* Keep your chest up and back neutral
* Lower your hips down, then stand back up
* Move at a steady, controlled pace

**Tips:**

* Breathe normally
* Focus on good form, not speed
* Stop if you feel pain or dizziness
